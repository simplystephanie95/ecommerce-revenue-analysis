# Business Insights

## Revenue Performance

The business generated **£10.67M in revenue across 19,960 orders** during the study period.

Average order value was **£534**, suggesting relatively large purchases compared with typical retail transactions.

---

## Seasonal Demand

Revenue demonstrates strong seasonality.

Sales increase significantly from September through November, peaking at **£1.51M in November**.

This pattern suggests strong holiday purchasing behaviour.

Businesses should prioritize inventory planning ahead of Q4.

---

## Geographic Revenue Distribution

The **United Kingdom accounts for more than 80% of total revenue**, indicating heavy reliance on the domestic market.

International markets such as **Netherlands, Ireland, Germany, and France** contribute smaller but meaningful revenue streams.

These regions present potential growth opportunities.

---

## Product Sales Patterns

Product analysis revealed two key product types driving revenue:

High-margin products  
Example: Regency Cake Stand 3 Tier

High-volume products  
Example: Paper Craft Little Birdie

This combination provides both consistent sales volume and strong revenue generation.

---

## Customer Behaviour

Customer segmentation shows a strong presence of repeat buyers.

Repeat purchasing behaviour suggests a loyal customer base and stable demand patterns.
